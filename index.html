Number Generation Game

Click the button to generate three random numbers and play the game!

Remaining Score: 500

Game Rules

1. Three random numbers between 1 and 9 will be generated.

2. Based on the numbers, your score will be updated according to these conditions:

  • All Numbers Same: If all three numbers are the same, you will get 1000 INR.
  • All Numbers Even or All Numbers Odd: If all three numbers are either even or odd, you will get 300 INR.
  • Numbers with a Difference of 1: If the numbers have a difference of 1 between them (e.g., 1, 2, 3), you will get 800 INR.
  • No Winning Conditions: If none of the conditions are met, you will not get any score.

3. In each round, 100 points will be deducted from your score.

4. If your score drops to 0 or below, the game ends and shows "Game Over".

Good luck and have fun!
